    About us.

    Alpha Enterprise Corporation was founded on June 1, 1975 by K.D. "Doug" Gamblin. Building on years of experience in the floor care industry, Doug's aim was to provide fast, excellent service for floor cleaning equipment.

    We have grown from one mobile service tech who assisted customers along the northern and central California coast to a fleet of techs who cover all of California and parts of Nevada. At Alpha, we believe in "getting it right the first time." All our service techs receive extensive training in floor care equipment maintenance and repairs.

    Our sales employees are trained in many types of floor care maintenance and are knowledgeable in procedures to care for vinyl (VCT), concrete and tile floors.
